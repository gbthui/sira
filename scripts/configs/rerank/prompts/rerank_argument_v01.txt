Given an argument and a document, rate how well the document serves as a COUNTER-ARGUMENT to the given argument.

Argument: {query}

Document: {document}

A good counter-argument DIRECTLY OPPOSES the argument's position. It may:
- Present evidence that contradicts the argument's claims
- Challenge the argument's reasoning or assumptions
- Argue for an alternative position on the same issue
- Acknowledge the argument's points but explain why they are outweighed

Rate from 0 to 100:
- 0: completely unrelated topic
- 1-20: same broad topic but does not engage with the argument's position
- 21-50: related to the topic and touches on the debate, but does not directly counter this specific argument
- 51-80: addresses the same issue and presents an opposing perspective, but only partially counters the specific claims made
- 81-100: directly and comprehensively argues against the position taken, engaging with the specific claims and providing counter-evidence or counter-reasoning

IMPORTANT: A document that AGREES with the argument should score LOW (0-30), even if it is topically similar. The goal is to find the opposing side of the debate, not supporting evidence.

Output only: {{"score": <integer>}}
